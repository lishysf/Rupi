Rupi (Smart Expense Tracker)

### 🔑 **Essential Dashboard Elements**

1. **Balance Overview**

   * Current balance (income – expenses).
   * Month-to-date balance progress.

2. **Income & Expense Summary**

   * Total income this week/month.
   * Total expenses this week/month.
   * Net savings (income – expenses).

3. **Category Breakdown**

   * Pie chart or bar chart showing expenses by category (Food, Transport, Bills, Entertainment, etc.).
   * Highlight top 3 categories.

4. **Daily/Weekly Trends**

   * Line chart of expenses over time.
   * Compare current week vs. last week.

5. **AI Insights (from LLaMA)**

   * **Summaries**: “Pengeluaran minggu ini Rp850.000, naik 15% dari minggu lalu.”
   * **Suggestions**: “Kurangi transportasi online 2x, bisa hemat Rp50.000/minggu.”
   * **Highlights**: “Kategori kopi sudah mencapai 70% dari budget bulanan.”

6. **Recent Transactions**

   * Simple, scrollable list.
   * Example: “Bensin Rp50.000 – Transport – 28 Aug.”

7. **Budget Tracking**

   * Monthly budget vs. actual spending.
   * Progress bar with color indicators (green = safe, red = overspending).

8. **Savings Goals**

   * Example: “Target tabungan laptop Rp10 juta, sudah tercapai 40%.”

9. **Financial Health Score**

   * Simple score (0–100) based on spending habits.
   * Gamified to encourage saving.
   * Mascot that changes expression based on the health score

   * Overspending warning.
   * Reminder if no transaction logged for a few days.
